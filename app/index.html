For Mentors

  • Understand your mentee’s personal goals
  • Get guidance on how to be an effective mentor

Features

For Mentees

  • Easy to use speech editor and audio recording
  • List of available mentors from your Toastmasters club
  • Mentor profiles with a summary of skills and experience
  • Invite additional mentors from outside your Toastmasters club
  • Request feedback on your speech or audio recording
  • Receive feedback aligned with your personal goals
  • Receive notifications when mentors provide feedback
  • Review progress in context of your personal goals

For Mentees

  • Mentor as few or as many members as you want
  • View your mentee’s personal goals
  • Provide upfront expectations for your mentees
  • Receive notifications when mentees request feedback
  • Guidance and examples on how to provide effective feedback

For Mentees

  • Easily keep track of who is mentoring who
  • See how often everyone communicates
  • Learn how satisfied mentor and mentees are with their relationship
  • Receive notifications when members become mentors
  • Receive notifications when more mentors are needed
  • Experienced members are automatically encouraged to become mentors
  • Simple monthly subscription
